Learning System

Train the full debate loop: research, planning, and delivery.

This page gives you a structured prep workflow and an AI coach entry point. Keep your arguments evidence-backed and easy to rebut under pressure.

Research

Find two supporting sources and one opposing source before building your opening statement.

Planning

Map your argument chain: claim -> evidence -> impact. Keep each chain under 25 seconds.

Delivery

Use pauses, clear signposting, and direct responses to rebuttals instead of repeating points.

AI Debate Coach

Enter your topic and get a draft argument structure. Connect your own API endpoint in assets/js/config.js for live AI responses.
