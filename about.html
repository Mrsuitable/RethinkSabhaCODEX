Origin Story

Rethink Sabha was created to make better arguments, not louder ones.

Built by a student creator, the platform focuses on learning how to research responsibly, frame stronger claims, and disagree without disrespect.

Mission

Raise the quality of civic and educational debate by making structure and evidence non-negotiable.

Who it is for

Students, first-time debaters, and young professionals who want confidence under disagreement.

What is next

Ranked sessions, mentor feedback loops, and source-quality scoring in prep workflows.

Creator Note

Rethink Sabha started as a small experiment by a teen builder in India who cared deeply about websites, research, and public speaking. The long-term goal is a platform where young people can practice reasoned disagreement with confidence and fairness.
